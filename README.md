# ASHA_Claim App Overview

The ASHA_Claim_Management_App is a cutting-edge mobile application is a digital platform designed for Auxiliary Nurse Midwives (ANMs) to manage ASHA (Accredited Social Health Activist) incentive claims, ensuring timely, accurate, and transparent payment processing to sustain ASHA motivation and improve health outcomes.

## Core Objectives

1. **Timely Payment**: Ensure ASHAs receive their incentives promptly
2. **Accuracy**: Minimize errors through source-level data entry
3. **Transparency**: Maintain clear records of all claims and approvals
4. **Motivation**: Sustain ASHA workforce engagement through reliable payment systems
5. **Health Indicator Achievement**: Support better health outcomes through motivated workforce

## Primary Users

- **ANMs (Auxiliary Nurse Midwives)**: Primary users who enter and manage ASHA claims
- **Block and District Officials**: For approval and monitoring
- **ASHAs**: Indirect beneficiaries who receive timely payments

## Core Modules and Functions

### 1. ASHA Profile Management Module
**Core Functions:**
- Registration and maintenance of ASHA worker profiles
- Personal details management (name, address, contact information)
- Village/area assignment details
- Employment status tracking
- Unique identification for each ASHA

### 2. Monthly Claims Entry Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Activity-wise claim entry**: Record each ASHA's activities performed during the month
- **Month-wise segregation**: Organize claims by specific months for systematic tracking
- **Activity categorization**: Simplified grouping of ASHA activities into predefined categories
- **Date stamping**: Automatic recording of entry dates
- **Multiple activity support**: Ability to enter multiple activities for single ASHA in one session

**Activity Categories Include:**
- Reproductive, Maternal, Newborn, Child Health and Adolescent (RMNCHA+N) activities
- Immunization support
- Family planning services
- Tuberculosis (TB) case identification and support
- Malaria and vector-borne disease activities
- Non-Communicable Disease (NCD) screening support
- Sanitation and hygiene promotion
- Community mobilization activities

### 3. Incentive Calculation Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Automatic calculation**: Computes incentives based on activities performed
- **Rate master integration**: Uses predefined rates for each activity type
- **Real-time calculation**: Shows calculated amounts as activities are entered
- **Multiple scheme support**: Handles incentives from various national health programs
- **Summary generation**: Provides total incentive amount per ASHA per month

### 4. Claims Submission and Approval Workflow Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Submit claims**: ANMs submit compiled monthly claims
- **Multi-level approval**: Support for hierarchical approval process
  - ANM level verification
  - Block level approval
  - District level final approval
- **Status tracking**: Real-time status of claims (Pending/Approved/Rejected/Modified)
- **Approval history**: Maintains complete audit trail of all approvals

### 5. Notification and Alert Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Push notifications to ANMs**: Alerts regarding:
  - Claim approval status
  - Modification requests
  - Payment processing updates
  - Deadline reminders for claim submission
- **In-app notifications**: Real-time updates within the application
- **SMS integration**: Text alerts for critical updates

### 6. Training Management Module
**Core Functions:**
- Training records for ASHAs
- Training attendance tracking
- Certification management
- Training schedule information
- Training-linked incentive tracking

### 7. ASHA Empowerment Schemes Module
**Core Functions:**
- Information about various empowerment initiatives
- Scheme eligibility tracking
- Benefits received tracking
- Documentation management for schemes

### 8. Data Validation and Error Prevention Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Source-level validation**: Real-time data validation during entry
- **Mandatory field checks**: Ensures all required information is captured
- **Duplicate detection**: Prevents duplicate entries for same activity
- **Range validation**: Ensures numeric values are within acceptable limits
- **Logical checks**: Validates relationships between different data fields

### 9. Reporting and Dashboard Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Monthly summary reports**: Consolidated view of all claims by month
- **ASHA-wise reports**: Individual performance and payment tracking
- **Activity-wise analysis**: Breakdown by activity categories
- **Pending claims dashboard**: Quick view of claims awaiting approval
- **Payment status reports**: Track disbursement status
- **Comparative analysis**: Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons

### 10. Modification and Correction Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Edit functionality**: Allows corrections to submitted claims before approval
- **Rejection handling**: Manage rejected claims with reasons
- **Resubmission workflow**: Process for correcting and resubmitting rejected claims
- **Modification tracking**: Maintains history of all changes made

### 11. Payment Tracking Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Payment processing status**: Track claims through payment pipeline
- **Bank details management**: ASHA bank account information
- **Payment confirmation**: Record successful disbursements
- **Payment history**: Complete record of all payments made
- **Reconciliation support**: Match claims with actual payments

### 12. Data Synchronization Module
**Core Functions:**
- **Offline capability**: Allow data entry without internet connectivity
- **Auto-sync**: Automatic synchronization when connection is available
- **Conflict resolution**: Handle data conflicts during synchronization
- **Backup and restore**: Ensure data integrity

## Simplified Claim Process Flow

1. **ANM enters ASHA activities** → Source-level data entry with validation
2. **System categorizes activities** → Automatic grouping by predefined categories
3. **System calculates incentives** → Based on activity rates
4. **ANM reviews and submits** → Final verification before submission
5. **Approval workflow** → Multi-tier approval process
6. **Notification sent** → ANM receives approval/modification alerts
7. **Payment processing** → Approved claims forwarded for disbursement
8. **Payment confirmation** → Final notification of successful payment
